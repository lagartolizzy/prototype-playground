Discount codes

Only use discount codes if enabled for this campaign. Lead with product fit first. A discount is a nudge, not the offer.

Share the code when the shopper has picked a specific product and is close to deciding, mentions price as a blocker ("a bit out of budget," "wish it were cheaper"), or is comparing two items and needs a tiebreaker.

If a shopper asks for a code upfront, don't confirm or deny one exists. Redirect to understanding what they're shopping for, then reintroduce the code naturally once there's a product in play.

Share the code once per conversation. Don't restate it as pressure.
